Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

This Privacy Policy explains how Blake Black LLC, doing business as BAGIDOR, collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you visit our website, contact us, follow us, or interact with our products and services.

Last updated: June 6, 2026

At a Glance

BAGIDOR is a consumer cigar storage brand. We use information to operate the website, respond to inquiries, support customers, improve our offerings, and communicate with people who choose to hear from us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, message content, social media handle, customer support details, and any other information you choose to share when contacting BAGIDOR.

If BAGIDOR offers online purchasing, promotions, wholesale inquiries, or account features in the future, we may collect order details, shipping information, billing information, transaction history, and related customer service information. Payment card details are typically processed by third-party payment providers and are not stored by us unless clearly stated at the point of collection.

We may also collect limited technical information automatically, such as browser type, device type, pages viewed, referring pages, approximate location derived from IP address, and interactions with our website. This helps us understand site performance and visitor interest.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to respond to questions, provide product support, process requests, maintain and improve the website, understand customer interest, protect against misuse, comply with legal obligations, and communicate about BAGIDOR when permitted.

We may use contact information to send updates, offers, or product news if you sign up for those communications or otherwise allow them. You can opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe instructions in the email or contacting us directly.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies, pixels, analytics tools, or similar technologies to keep the site working, remember preferences, measure traffic, understand performance, and support marketing or social media features.

You can usually adjust cookie settings through your browser. Blocking cookies may affect how some website features work.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information for money. If future analytics, advertising, or marketing tools are considered a "sale" or "sharing" of personal information under applicable law, we will provide any required notices and opt-out choices.

We may share information with service providers that help us operate the business, such as website hosting, email, analytics, payment processing, shipping, customer support, advertising, or fraud prevention providers.

We may also share information when required by law, to protect rights and safety, to enforce our terms, or as part of a business transaction such as a merger, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets.

5. Social Media and Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party websites and social media platforms, including Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube, and Blake Black LLC websites. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies, not this Privacy Policy.

If you interact with BAGIDOR through social media, the platform may provide us with information such as your public profile, comments, messages, or engagement with our posts, depending on your settings and the platform's rules.

6. Data Retention

We keep personal information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including customer support, business records, legal compliance, fraud prevention, and dispute resolution.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. No website, transmission, or storage system can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

8. Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information you have provided to us by contacting us. We may need to verify your request and may retain information where permitted or required by law.

You may opt out of marketing emails by following the unsubscribe instructions in those messages or by contacting us at hello@blake.black.

9. State Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have additional privacy rights under applicable state privacy laws. These may include the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion or correction, receive a copy of certain information, or opt out of certain uses of personal information.

To make a privacy request, contact us using the information below. We will respond as required by applicable law.

10. Children's Privacy

BAGIDOR is intended for adults and is not directed to children or anyone under the legal age to purchase tobacco products. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
